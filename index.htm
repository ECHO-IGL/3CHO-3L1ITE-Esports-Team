Welcome to 3CHO 3L1TE Esport

We are a professional christian esports organization dedicated to excellence in competitive gaming. Our team consists of talented players who compete at the highest level in various esports titles. Join us on our journey to victory and experience the thrill of esports with 3CHO 3L1TE Esport!
3CHO 3L1TE Esport was founded in 2026 by G0at3t and co-founder Mattdlg. They are known for the team's commitment to fostering a positive and inclusive gaming community, as well as their dedication to supporting their players both on and off the virtual battlefield. With a strong emphasis on teamwork, sportsmanship, and faith, 3CHO 3L1TE Esport strives to create an environment where players can thrive and achieve their full potential in the world of competitive gaming.
Our team competes in a variety of esports titles, including Fortnite, Rocket League, and Valorant. We are proud to have a roster of skilled players who represent our organization with integrity and passion. Whether it's dominating the competition in Fortnite, showcasing our skills in Rocket League, or strategizing in Valorant, 3CHO 3L1TE Esport is committed to excellence and making a positive impact in the esports community. Join us on our journey to success and be a part of the 3CHO 3L1TE Esport family!

Our Values

At 3CHO 3L1TE Esport, we are committed to upholding the highest standards of sportsmanship, integrity, and faith. We believe in creating a positive and inclusive environment where all players can thrive and achieve their full potential.

We value teamwork and collaboration, recognizing that success in esports is not just about individual skill but also about working together as a cohesive unit. We encourage our players to support and uplift each other, fostering a sense of camaraderie and unity within our organization.

  • Sportsmanship: We believe in fair play, respect for opponents, and graciousness in victory or defeat.
  • Integrity: We are committed to honesty, transparency, and ethical behavior in all aspects of our organization.
  • Faith: We strive to incorporate our Christian values into our gaming community, promoting positivity and kindness.

Join us at 3CHO 3L1TE Esport as we continue to grow and make a positive impact in the esports community. Together, we can achieve greatness and inspire others to pursue their passion for gaming while upholding our core values.

Games We Play

At 3CHO 3L1TE Esport, we compete in a variety of popular esports titles, including:

  • Fortnite: A battle royale game where players fight to be the last one standing on a shrinking map.
  • Rocket League: A high-octane vehicular soccer game where players control rocket-powered cars to score goals.
  • Valorant: A tactical first-person shooter where players compete in teams to complete objectives and eliminate opponents.

Our team is dedicated to mastering these games and competing at the highest level. We are constantly training, strategizing, and improving our skills to stay competitive in the ever-evolving world of esports. Join us on our journey to success as we represent 3CHO 3L1TE Esport in these exciting games and strive for victory!
